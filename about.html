About Our Factory

KEDA Furniture is a professional steel office furniture manufacturer with over 20 years of production experience, supplying high-quality products to customers worldwide.



We specialize in steel filing cabinets, lockers, mobile pedestals, office desks and storage solutions. All products are produced with strict quality standards and exported to Europe, America, Asia and Africa. OEM & ODM services are available.
